ABOUT US.

Anyone with a digital camera can take photographs according to his or her whims and fancies. However the most salient feature of digital photography is that, one can see the photographs instantly on a computer or mobile camera without any delay. The popularity of mobile camera & digital camera have made thousands of people to enhance their photography skills and exhibit them in social media websites


Still, the print versions enjoy great popularity among the common folks. Gemini Photo Studio & Stores is specially designed for those who want to share their photographs in printed versions with family and friends. We are in existence since 1951 and have been servicing ever since then with always updating our self with the latest printing technologies. We have got some unbelievable offers for those who wish to send the photographs anywhere in India. What we do is that, we take prints with our state- of-the- art digital machines and send it to the mentioned addresses in your order.

All you need to do is to register, upload your photographs make your payments.

WHO ARE WE?

JB Photo Studio & Stores is a professional studio & lab delivering happiness and smiles to every family. We help you to print your mobile snaps into photo prints and many personalized photo gifts to treasure memories of your family, friends and loved ones. Now you have a reason to print those thousand snaps from your mobile gallery to cherish past moments and smile again.

JBPhoto Studio & Stores provides various print sizes which matched your requirements. We use silver halide printing technology and the photo paper used for printing is Kodak or Fuji as per the availability of stock.

Each and every print order passes through several manual processes. The cropping, colour correction, and printing are done under strict supervision of our quality control people and we have been successful in giving the maximum available print output for the quality of images provided by our customers.

We provide the reasonable price prevailing in the online photo printing industry and the best quality products.

Please visit our pricing page for knowing the details of size, rates and shipping charges.
